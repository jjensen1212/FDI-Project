FDI QuickReferece Website

The purpose of this website is to inform policymakers, the academic community, and the public in general about the virtues and attributes of one of the key elements of the global economy: foreign direct investment.

In a world that has witnessed a trend towards retrenchment and nationalism over the past years, it is important to emphasize the benefits that economic openness and international co-operation has brought to humanity. Those benefits include higher economic growth, the flow of people and ideas, an unprecedented reduction of poverty and increase in global wellbeing, among others.Fostering economic activity through foreign direct investment (FDI) is a fundamental component of such economic openness and international co-operation.

This website provides a brief and compelling overview of the concept of FDI and why it is important for development, including:

  • The main recipients and the main sources of FDI over the past decade (FDISources).
  • The link between FDI and development (FDI-Devel).
  • A world map with FDI figures at a glance (FDIMap).
