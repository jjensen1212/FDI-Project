FDI QuickReferece Website

Purpose of this website

The purpose of this website is to inform the academic community, in particular students about the virtues and attributes of one of the key elements of the global economy: foreign direct investment (FDI).

In a world that has witnessed a trend towards retrenchment and nationalism over the past years, it is important to emphasize the benefits of economic openness and international cooperation. Those benefits include economic growth, the flow of people and ideas, an unprecedented reduction of poverty, among others.Fostering economic activity through foreign direct investment is a fundamental component of such economic openness and international co-operation.

Graphs and figures

This website provides a brief and compelling overview of:

  • The concept of FDI and why it is important.
  • The main recipients and the main sources of FDI over the past decade (FDISources).
  • A world map with FDI figures at a glance (FDIMap).

    • Data used

    The figures presented are drawn from the World Development Indicators data set of the World Bank.
