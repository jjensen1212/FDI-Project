FDI inflows and outflows in G20 countries and in the main global regions

In this page, we show compiled data on inbound and outbound FDI for G20 countries and for the major global regions. The data covers 2012-2021 and comprises figures in terms of the share of GDP and in current US dollars. The tables and charts allow for selecting specific G20 countries and downloading the data, which is sourced from the World Bank, World Development Indicators. The tables and charts have been generated with Datawrapper.
